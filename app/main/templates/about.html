About Office Hour Scheduler

Welcome to the Office Hour Scheduler application! We've created this platform to streamline the process of booking appointments between students and teachers.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify the appointment scheduling process, making it easier for students to connect with their teachers for one-on-one help, feedback, and guidance.

Key Features

  • Easy appointment booking and management
  • Automated notifications and reminders
  • Streamlined feedback and reporting
  • Intuitive interface for both students and teachers
